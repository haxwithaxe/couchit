About Couchit

Couchit allow you to easily build and maintain your website.

Post your content and let's go.

Couchit is for now in beta, but already propose you:

  • a simple and intuitive user interface
  • the possibility to create and edit page
  • the possibility to choose your site url
  • an easyway to choose your website colorscheme
  • an easy integration of paste from Friendpaste
  • ...

An the best is coming...

How is build couchit ?

  • No SQL database. We use the new and powerfull document oriented database Apache CouchDB.
  • Werkzeug, a micro web framework

Who ?

Couchit has been produced by Enki Multimedia.

Privacy>

There is no logging on Couchit. Cookie is only used to store your preferences. Period.
